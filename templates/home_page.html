About Unizulu Clinic

Dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services to the University of Zululand community

Your Health is Our Priority

Established in 1995, Unizulu Clinic has been serving the University of Zululand community for over 25 years. We are committed to providing high-quality, accessible healthcare services to students, faculty, and staff.

Our team of experienced healthcare professionals includes registered nurses, nurse practitioners, and visiting physicians who specialize in campus health and student wellness.

We understand the unique health challenges faced by students and are dedicated to providing compassionate, confidential care in a welcoming environment.

Our Mission

To enhance the academic experience by promoting health and wellness through accessible, high-quality healthcare services and education.

Our Vision

To be the leading campus health service provider in South Africa, recognized for excellence in student healthcare and wellness innovation.
